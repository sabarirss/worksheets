Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

GleeGrow - Happy Learning!

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

GleeGrow is an educational platform for children ages 4–10+. We take the privacy and safety of children extremely seriously. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service, and we never sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (provided by parents)

  • Parent email address — for account login and essential communications
  • Parent name — for account identification
  • Password — stored encrypted via Firebase Authentication

1.2 Child Profile Information (provided by parents)

  • Child's first name — for personalization (e.g., "Great job, Emma!")
  • Child's date of birth — to determine age-appropriate content levels

1.3 Learning Progress Data (generated during use)

  • Worksheet completion status and scores
  • Assessment results and assigned learning levels
  • Weekly assignment progress
  • Time spent on worksheets (when timer is used)

1.4 What We Do NOT Collect

  • No location data or GPS information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • No social media information
  • No browsing history outside our platform
  • No cookies for tracking or advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  1. Authentication — verifying parent identity and securing accounts
  2. Content personalization — showing age-appropriate worksheets for each child
  3. Progress tracking — saving completed work and showing learning progress to parents
  4. Weekly assignments — generating progressive worksheets at the right difficulty level

We explicitly do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing to children
  • Selling or sharing with third parties
  • Training AI/ML models beyond the in-browser handwriting recognition
  • Behavioral profiling or cross-site tracking

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore database and Authentication), which provides enterprise-grade security
  • Passwords are hashed and salted — we never store plaintext passwords
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Handwriting recognition runs entirely in the browser using TensorFlow.js — no handwriting images are sent to any server
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict data access to authenticated users accessing only their own data

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for secure user login (Google LLC)
  • Cloud Firestore — for data storage (Google LLC)
  • Firebase Hosting — for serving the website (Google LLC)
  • TensorFlow.js — for in-browser handwriting recognition (runs locally, no data sent)

Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Firebase is certified under major compliance frameworks including SOC 1/2/3 and ISO 27001.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

5.1 Parental Control

  • Only parents/guardians can create accounts and add child profiles
  • Children cannot create accounts independently
  • Parents have full visibility and control over all child data

5.2 COPPA Compliance (United States)

For users under 13 years of age, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act:

  • Parental consent is obtained before any data collection
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time
  • We collect only the minimum information necessary

5.3 GDPR Compliance (European Union)

For users under 16 years of age in the EU:

  • Parental consent is required per GDPR Article 8
  • Data processing is based on legitimate interest (providing educational services) and consent
  • Data minimization principles are strictly followed

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you and your children
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate data through your account settings
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to any processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent — at any time by deleting your account

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Upon account deletion, all data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • No data is retained for analytics, training, or any other purpose after deletion

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your data rights:

Email: contact@gleegrow.com

Response Time: We will respond to all requests within 30 days as required by GDPR Article 12.

Summary

  • We collect minimal data (name, DOB, email) only to run the service
  • Your data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising
  • Handwriting recognition runs in your browser — nothing is sent to servers
  • Parents have full control over all child data
  • You can delete everything permanently at any time
